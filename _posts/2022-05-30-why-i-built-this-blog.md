As I grew older, I felt more and more ignorant. I desperately need to absorb more knowledge and ponder deeper questions, and this blog will be a powerful tool to help me achieve those goals. This blog will drive me to record what I read, think, and do. Writing is thinking.

My first few posts are probably more reading notes. Reading is the basis for thinking; if I'm going to think more, the more I have to read. As my reading increases, I should gradually be able to write more original posts. I also believe that if I get richer in ideas, I will surely find ways to put those ideas into practice to change the world.

Regarding the note-taking method, I have tried many, and so far I have not figured out the most suitable one, so I think the important thing is maybe not the method, but the thinking. What you read, what comes to mind from it, just write it down.
